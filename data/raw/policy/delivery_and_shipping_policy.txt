Delivery and Shipping Policy

1. Standard delivery timelines range between 3 to 7 days, depending on the customer’s location.
2. If an order is marked as delivered but the customer has not received it:
   - A delivery investigation must be initiated
   - Resolution may include a replacement or a refund
3. Delivery delays caused by logistics partners may qualify for partial refunds or delivery credits at the platform’s discretion.
4. If an incorrect item is delivered, the customer is eligible for a free replacement with return pickup.
