Customer Support Policies
Applies to all orders placed on the platform
Last updated: December 2026


1. Refund Policy

1. Refunds are issued only after verifying the order status and payment confirmation.
2. If an item is damaged, defective, or incorrect, the customer is eligible for either a full refund or a replacement, based on availability.
3. Refunds are always processed to the original payment method used at checkout.
4. Once approved, refunds usually reflect within:
   - 3 to 5 business days for UPI, wallets, and debit/credit cards
   - 5 to 7 business days for net banking transactions
5. If a refund is delayed beyond the stated timeline, the ticket must be escalated to the finance team for manual verification.

Non-refundable cases:
- Items marked as non-returnable
- Orders cancelled after shipment unless the item is damaged, defective, or incorrect


2. Payment Issues Policy

1. If payment is deducted but the order is not confirmed, the deducted amount is automatically refunded.
2. In the case of duplicate or double payment, only the excess amount is refunded.
3. EMI or bank-side payment failures may require the customer to contact their bank after verification from the platform.
4. All payment-related issues must be treated as high-priority tickets.
5. No payment-related ticket should be auto-closed without confirmation of resolution.


3. Delivery and Shipping Policy

1. Standard delivery timelines range between 3 to 7 days, depending on the customer’s location.
2. If an order is marked as delivered but the customer has not received it:
   - A delivery investigation must be initiated
   - Resolution may include a replacement or a refund
3. Delivery delays caused by logistics partners may qualify for partial refunds or delivery credits at the platform’s discretion.
4. If an incorrect item is delivered, the customer is eligible for a free replacement with return pickup.


4. Cancellation Policy

1. Orders can be cancelled before shipment without any penalty.
2. Orders cancelled after shipment are generally not eligible for cancellation unless:
   - The item is damaged
   - The wrong item was delivered
3. Refunds for cancelled orders follow the standard refund processing timeline.


5. Return Policy

1. Returns are accepted within 7 to 10 days of delivery unless otherwise specified on the product page.
2. Items must be unused, undamaged, and returned in original packaging.
3. Return pickup is subject to service availability at the customer’s address.
4. After the returned item is received and verified, a refund or replacement is processed accordingly.


6. Account and Security Policy

1. Accounts may be temporarily locked after multiple failed login attempts.
2. Suspicious or unauthorized activity requires immediate identity verification and a mandatory password reset.
3. Unauthorized orders must always be escalated for manual review.
4. No account-level changes should be made without proper customer identity confirmation.


7. Escalation Guidelines

The following issues must not be auto-resolved and require human review or manager escalation:
- Payment failures
- Unauthorized transactions
- Legal or compliance-related complaints
- High-severity delivery disputes
- Tickets containing angry, abusive, or threatening language


8. Communication Guidelines

1. All communication with customers must remain polite, professional, and clear.
2. Do not guarantee outcomes that depend on third-party verification or external services.
3. Avoid sharing internal workflows, internal timelines, or operational details.
4. Never ask customers for sensitive information such as OTPs, passwords, or card details.


9. Ticket Closure Rules

1. Tickets should be closed only after a clear resolution has been provided and communicated to the customer.
2. If follow-up action is required, the ticket must remain open until completion.
3. Every closed ticket must include a clear and concise resolution note for audit purposes.
